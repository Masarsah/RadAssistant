• Female patients should not be pregnant. Please inform the Nuclear Medicine staff and the doctor if you are pregnant or think you are pregnant. Avoid becoming pregnant for 3 months after radioiodine dose. (We will proceed if it medically necessary).
  • Stop breast-feeding 3 weeks before receiving radioiodine dose. Discard the milk after radioiodine dosing.
  • Please don’t bring your kids to the Nuclear Medicine Department.
  • Do Blood test in the laboratory on Sunday before dosing.
  • For 4 weeks: Stop thyroxine prior to your radioiodine dosing and until after your scan is completed or if radioiodine therapy is to be given until day of discharge from ward.
  • Don’t stop other medications.
  • For duration stated: Avoid interfering materials listed in attached leaflet A.

  • For 7 days: Go on low in iodine diet listed in attached leaflet C for 7 days prior to your ￼￼￼￼radioiodine dosing and continue until after your scan is completed.
  • For 4 hours on Day 3 only: Full fast (no food and water) after breakfast before oral radioiodine dosing. For 2 hours after dosing, continue full fast. Do not fast on other days (Day 1, Day 2 and Day 5).
  • For 3 days after dosing: drink plenty of water and empty your bladder frequently.
  • For 3 days after dosing: take Laxative (Dulcolax) at bedtime.
  • Before coming for scan: Empty bowel and bladder, change diapers (if applicable).
